How this contest works

You'll be given real-world scenarios from NextWealth's work. For each one, you write an AI prompt, test it in any AI tool, then paste both your prompt and the AI's response back here.

For each question, follow these 6 steps:

1

Read the scenario

Understand the context, data, and task before writing anything.

2

Copy the data

Use the Copy button to copy the context/data from the question.

3

Open an AI tool

Open ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Copilot in another tab.

4

Write & test your prompt

Paste the data + write your prompt instructions. Run it and see the response.

5

Paste prompt + response

Come back here. Paste your full prompt AND the AI response you got.

6

Submit & continue

Click Save & Next. Scoring happens in the background — no waiting!

💡 Tips for a good prompt:

  • Give the AI a clear role — "You are a catalog QA reviewer..."
  • Always include the context/data inside your prompt
  • Specify the exact output format — table, JSON, bullet points
  • Add constraints — "use only the provided context", "under 80 words"
  • For complex tasks — break it into numbered steps
